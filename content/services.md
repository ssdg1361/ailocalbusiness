## Website Design {#website-design}

A website is still the single most valuable digital asset most local businesses own. Your Google Business Profile, your AI search visibility, your ad campaigns — they all point back to it. If the site is slow, dated, or built for the wrong audience, everything else underperforms.

I build small, fast, conversion-ready sites for local service businesses — not 30-page agency rebuilds. The goal: a site that loads quickly on a phone in a customer's driveway, surfaces what you actually do, and tells Google and AI crawlers exactly who you are.

- **Custom builds for local service businesses** — Designed around the customer journey of "search, click, call" rather than corporate-style multi-page sprawl
- **Schema markup baked in from day one** — LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, and Review schema configured so search engines and AI models can read your site cleanly
- **Core Web Vitals performance** — Fast load, mobile-first, accessible. The metrics Google actually uses to rank you in 2026
- **Tied into your Google Business Profile** — NAP consistency, schema, links, and tracking working together so the site amplifies your local presence rather than fighting it
- **Built for AI crawlers, not just Google** — Clean semantic HTML, llms.txt-ready, content structured to be cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews

I'll be honest about scope: I'm not a 12-person agency. I'm one person who builds sites that work hard for small operators. If you need a 50-page enterprise site, I'm not your guy. If you need a sharp, fast, well-optimized site that helps you book more local work, I am.
